Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the course materials?

All course materials, including notes, videos, and resources, are freely accessible through our course detail pages. Simply click on any course to view its content.

Are the courses completely free?

Yes! CourseCatalyst curates and organizes existing free resources from across the web. We don't charge for access to any materials or courses.

Can I suggest new courses or resources?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for new courses, resources, or improvements. Use the contact form above or specify "Resource Suggestion" as your topic.

Do you provide certificates?

CourseCatalyst is a resource aggregator. Certificates would be provided by the original course creators on platforms like YouTube, Coursera, etc.
